Privacy Policy

15-Puzzle-15 – 3x3 & 4x4 – developed by Dominik Kaiser

1. General Information

Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app Slide Puzzle – 3x3 & 4x4. The app is designed to provide a classic number sliding puzzle game and does not collect personal data from users.

2. Use of Game Center

The app uses Apple Game Center to provide features such as:

  • Achievements
  • Leaderboards

When using Game Center, Apple may process personal data according to Apple's own privacy policy. For more information, please visit: apple.com/legal/privacy

The developer of this app does not have access to any Game Center data.

3. Data Stored on the Device

The app may store certain information locally on your device, such as:

  • Game progress
  • Statistics
  • Best scores

This information is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to the developer.

4. No Data Sharing

The app does not collect, store, or share personal data with third parties.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

The developer may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the app or its official listing.
